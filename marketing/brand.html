Usage Rules

Keep it consistent. A few things to remember.

Do

  • Use the lockup or icon as provided
  • Write "worktale" in lowercase monospace
  • Keep generous clear space around the logo
  • Use on dark backgrounds for best contrast
  • Use the solid black icon on light backgrounds

Don't

  • Capitalize as "WorkTale" or "Worktale"
  • Hyphenate as "work-tale"
  • Change the icon colors or shape
  • Add effects like shadows or gradients to the logo
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
